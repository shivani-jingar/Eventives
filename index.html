About Us

For any business, it is very important that an exhibition is conducted that shows all the products and services that are offered by them to their target customers. This process of holding up exhibitions are also called as exhibition branding. For most of the brands, participating in an exhibition or a trade show is seen to act as a ladder to success. These exhibitions provide an excellent platform for these company to do effective business promotions and brand building both at the same time.

Onsite display is a very important aspect of any promotional event that is organized by a business. These displays are needed to be dealt with much care and professionalism as in the end, these are what defines the product in front of the people. For onsite promotion, there is a great need for professional help as getting the idea that is right for the promotions is not an easy task.

Locations

Exhibition Places

Hotel Sayaji

A major attraction

Sayajigunj is an area in the western side of Vadodara City in the state of Gujarat in India. Vadodara Railway Station and Central State Transport Bus depot is located in Sayajigung, which is why it is referred as a gate way to Vadodara city. This area has attraction of youngsters, due to the fact area being close to University; Some Major Hotels are also located in this area. Mostly, area is covered with stock brokers offices, hotels for stay, restaurants, banks, commercial Complex and University.

Navlakhi Ground

A wide open space

Navlakhi ground is a fair ground & Park located in Vadodara,IndiaTop Organizers hosting events at Navlakhi Ground include JDC EVENTS,Vadodara Chamber Of Commerce & Industry and more.

Hotel Grand Mercury

Luxurious spacing

The iconic Surya Palace hotel is now a Grand Mercure, an ideal choice for exhibition and art seeking persons for business and leisure alike. The hotel is a mere 2 min drive from the railway station and 10 min drive from the airport. Popular city attractions like MS University, Sayaji Garden, Baroda Museum and Laxmi Vilas Palace are a 5 min drive away.
